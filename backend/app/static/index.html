GrimDM

Voice AI Dungeon Master

How to Play

Speak naturally to the Dungeon Master. Describe what your character does, asks, or examines. The DM responds with voice, rolls dice, and controls the world.

⚔
Act & Explore

"I search the room for hidden doors"

"I walk toward the bridge"

💬
Talk to NPCs

"I ask the innkeeper about the curse"

"I try to persuade the troll to let me pass"

⚔
Fight

"I attack the creature with my sword"

"I try to dodge and strike back"

🎲
Use Items

"I drink the healing potion"

"What's in my inventory?"

Pro Tips

  • You can interrupt the DM mid-sentence — just start talking
  • Say "look around" for detailed scene descriptions
  • The DM may challenge you to gesture battles — show moves with your camera
  • Your choices affect NPC relationships and story outcomes
  • Say "what are my options?" if you're stuck

Microphone & camera access required for full experience
